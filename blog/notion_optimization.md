This checklist covers the structural "blocks" that bridge the gap between a simple blog and an AEO-optimized documentation powerhouse.
🛠️ The Roll 0.2 Notion Launch Checklist
1. The "Atomic" Header (AEO & Scannability)
TL;DR Callout: Is there a Callout block at the very top with a 🚀 icon? It must contain the "3x Throughput" result in bold.
Quick Links: Are there GitHub and Notion links immediately visible within the first 100 words?
2. Interactive Technical Depth (Engagement & Dwell Time)
Toggle Lists: Are the "Shrink/Expand" and "VRAM Management" details tucked into Toggle blocks? (Keep the page short, but the data deep).
Code Snippets: Did you include a copy-pasteable YAML or Python code block for the cluster_config? (This drives "Utility" value).
Visual Proof: Do your charts have Alt-Text descriptions in the "Caption" field? (Crucial for Google Image Search).
3. Data & Authority (SEO & Trust)
Comparison Table: Is your benchmark data in a Notion Table (not an image)? AI crawlers can "read" table cells but often ignore text inside images.
Hardware Specs: Does the "Experimental Setup" section explicitly mention 160-GPU Cluster and Qwen3-235B-A22B? (Signals authority to the algorithm).
4. The "Living Doc" Elements (Retention)
Future Roadmap: Is the Agentic LoRA Training feature listed in a "Coming Soon" section or a Database view?
Backlinks: Have you linked this post to your main Notion Workspace or a "Research" landing page?
5. Meta-Optimization (Final Polish)
Public Access: Is "Search Engine Indexing" toggled ON in Notion’s Share settings? (Otherwise, Google can't find it).
Slug Customization: Is the URL clean? (e.g., notion.site/roll-0-2-agentic-rl-throughput).
